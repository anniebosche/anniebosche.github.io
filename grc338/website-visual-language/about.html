Our Story

Sunday Coffee is for Coffee, Community and Comradarie. We’ve been an anchor on the corner of Bean and Ocean for 17 years.

It’s a place that’s fostered relationships within our community.

The owner and her husband have know their customers well, as she works everyday in the store for the last 17 years.

The community fondly visits Sunday Coffee Co as we continue to offer the fresh roasted beans and expert service that has made their coffee a daily staple for many. Years of experience and devotion to the coffee business gives Sunday the opportunity to continue in a neighborhood that appreciates their eclectic ambiance.
